Property Details

Tall and elegant, Aspen at Consort Place is a striking new 63-storey tower with incredible city-wide views, poised to be one of the largest in the already iconic Canary Wharf skyline. Part of Consort Place, a new address in the heart of London’s famed financial centre, residents and tenants at Aspen can take advantage of immediately local amenities such as cafes, bars, a health centre and a fully restored 1860 pub, but also incredible in-building amenities serviced by the international Dorsett Hotel, one of Asia’s fastest growing hospitality groups.

The building’s facilities include a 24-hour concierge service, gym, steam room and sauna, vitality pool, winter garden and terrace, skylounge on the 63rd floor, children’s play area, private screening room and a private dining and wine room. Of course, the real selling point are the views from the Aspen property, quite simply, incredible, stretching right across The City, London and beyond.

With such incredible on-site facilities, we expect these apartments to be trophy assets, with firm appeal to tenants. Aspen is a short walk from Canary Wharf Underground station, (Jubilee Line) and connected to the Elizabeth Line which reaches Bond Street in 13 mins. The specification is ultra-lux, as you would expect from such a high-calibre development and the interiors striking and characterful, put together by world renowned designers Hirsch Bedner Associates.

LOCATION

Aspen enjoys a prime riverside position in Canary Wharf, along the Marsh Wall on the Isle of Dogs and is connected to virtually every type of London transportation there is; nine minutes’ walk from Canary Wharf Underground (Jubilee Line), four mins from South Quay DLR, the Elizabeth Line accessible in a 15 minute walk where the commute time to Prime Central London (Bond Street) is an astonishing 13 minutes - as well as easy access to the Thames Clippers/Uber Boats.

Unbeknown to most, Canary Wharf is also a major retail destination; the third largest shopping centre in London with over 120 stores across five vibrant malls. The famous Billingsgate Market also is part of the neighbourhood and London City Airport is only a 14-min drive or a 14-min journey on the DLR.

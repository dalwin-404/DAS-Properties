PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 1st January 2025.

Last Updated: 13th May 2025.

DAS Properties and Investment Limited (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information when you use our website, engage our services or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Name, contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Identification documents (e.g., passport, ID card)
  • Financial details (for investment verification)
  • Investment preferences and objectives
  • Usage data from our website (via cookies or analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Deliver real estate investment services
  • Conduct identity verification (KYC)
  • Process transactions and manage investor accounts
  • Communicate important updates and offers
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Legal and financial partners for compliance
  • Developers and property managers for investment execution
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., KYC processors, IT support)

All third parties are contractually bound to protect your information.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures, including encryption and secure data storage, to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored outside your home country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to maintain data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access, update, or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, please contact: info@daspropertiesinvest.co.uk

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and collect anonymous usage data. You can modify cookie settings in your browser.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We recommend reviewing it regularly for any changes.

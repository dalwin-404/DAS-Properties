Property Details

An excellent property investment opportunity in the UK, The Triangle is a striking development of 99 apartments overlooking the River Stour and neighbouring Victoria Park in the boom town of Ashford, Kent. Ideally located, directly opposite Ashford’s flagship leisure centre Elwick Place, the development is only a 12-minute walk from Ashford International Station. Choose from studios, one, two and three-bedroomed apartments with river and parkland views. The development is directly connected to the town centre via a footbridge to the north, gaining direct access to Elwick Place and also Ashford College. Every convenience is a short walk away, including Ashford International Station, the town’s major retail centres – including the designer outlet village – and an array of supermarkets. Parking is included with two-and three-bedroom apartments and the development is scheduled for completion in Q1 2023.

Nearly 30% of households in Ashford comprise just one person and 40% are families with children. 18-39 year olds make up 70% of the tenant age profile in Ashford; working professionals and college students who want to live more centrally in Ashford town centre, close to amenities and transport connections. The rental market looks to be further shaken up by the approval of a £250million major film production hub for streaming giants such as Netflix and Amazon, known as Ashford International Studios.

LOCATION

Ashford was home to the first High Speed rail link in the UK, connecting the town with London St Pancras in 36 minutes and 29 to London Stratford. It is also a major terminus for Eurostar, directly connected to three European capitals; Brussels in 1h 32 mins, Paris in 1h 52 mins and Amsterdam in 3hrs 50 mins.

With a 24% growth in population over 20 years to 2031, it is one of the fastest growing populations in the south east, a trend only increased by the pandemic as people search for space outside of London, whilst also being unwilling to compromise on commute. Ashford offers both hence why it is presents an attractive UK property investment opportunity.

Attracting a growing number of businesses because of its low employee costs and excellent international rail connection to Europe, has had a knock-on effect on its housing market with strong demand for new homes, from both the local marketing and those migrating to Ashford.

Ashford has undergone intensive investment and regeneration to its public infrastructure, including a new completed motorway junction and more recently the approval of a mixed-use estate to create a world class film studio over 240,000 sq ft. Its designer shopping village recently completed a £90 million extension; home to over 130 premium brands.
